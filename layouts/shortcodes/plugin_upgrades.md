(For versions 2.0.0 and above)

- Go to Admin > Modules > Plugin Manager.
- Check each of the installed plugins (the Status indicator will be green, and in 2.2.1 and above, they will be at the top of the list).
  - Click the entry to open the right hand side infobox.
  - If the button "Upgrade Available" shows, click it.  Click through to complete the upgrade.
