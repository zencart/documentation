## What is a PM? 

The Zen Cart forum offers the capability for users to message each other. This feature is called  private messaging or PM. 

## What are the rules of PM? 
Private messages are subject to the [Zen Cart Code of Conduct](/user/zen_cart_forum/code_of_conduct/).  The [do's and don'ts list](/user/zen_cart_forum/dos_and_donts/) also provides some guidance. 

## How do I send a PM? 

There are two ways:

- Open the member's profile (click their username in a post, or find them in the
  [members directory](https://www.zen-cart.com/members)) and click the "Message"
  button.
- Go directly to [Compose Message](https://www.zen-cart.com/messages/compose) and
  search for the person you want to message.

The same formatting toolbar used in the forum is available when composing a PM,
including the image icon for pasting a link or uploading a picture directly - see
[Attachments](/user/zen_cart_forum/attachments/) for details.
