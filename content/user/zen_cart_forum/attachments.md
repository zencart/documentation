## How do I attach an image? 

- Reply to the post you want to add an image to (or click Post New Thread if it's a new thread).

- At the bottom of the post, click the Go Advanced button

- Click the Manage Attachments button.  A window will pop up.

- Click Add Files, then choose your file.  Click Upload. 

- The attachment will now show at the bottom of the popup window with a checkbox.  Click the Done button at the bottom.  The popup will now disappear. 

- Click Submit Reply at the bottom, and your attachment will be at the bottom of your post.
